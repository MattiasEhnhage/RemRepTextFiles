Variable compensation is conditional on the fact that defined and measurable criteria are met. 
At the beginning of each year, the Board and the Compensation Committee determine the criteria and 
target levels, which are deemed to be relevant for the coming measurement period. For 2024 
was 85 percent of variable compensation linked to financial criteria and 15 
percent to individual non-financial criteria.
The performance criteria of the CEO's variable remuneration 
aims to promote and realize the company's business strategy and performance as well as 
Sustainable long -term interests.
