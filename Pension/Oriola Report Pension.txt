 4 President & CEO’s remuneration 
The main components of the President & CEO’s total remuneration may include a base 
salary and short - and long- term incentives. If targets are met, over half of the President & 
CEO’s total remuneration is based on variable remuneration, with more emphasis on the 
long- term incentives. The compensation components may consist of cash, shares and/or 
options . 
The President & CEO’s current remuneration and detailed pay mix description is given on the 
company’s website.
