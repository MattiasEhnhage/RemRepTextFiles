Pension terms 
Senior executives shall be entitled to pension benefits consisting of retirement pension and 
premium exemption, that shall be premium defined, and health insurance, that shall be bene-
fit defined. Variable remuneration shall not be pension qualifying income, unless required by 
mandatory collective agreement provisions applicable for the senior executives at the pay-
ment date (in which case necessary adjustment shall be made of the variable remuneration so 
that cost neutrality is achieved for the company). The pension benefits for senior executives 
shall not exceed 33 per cent of the annual remuneration (fixed and variable, respectively), in-
cluding payment for vacation.
