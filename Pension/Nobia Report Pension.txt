4 (5) Other benefits 
Other benefits can include, inter alia, life insurance, health insurance and company car. For the 
President and other senior executives, other benefits cannot exceed 10 percent of the fixed annual cash salary. The President and other senior executives are further entitled to benefits that can be offered to other employees at any given moment.
