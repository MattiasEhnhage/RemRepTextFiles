benefits to senior executives are either defined -contribution or defined -benefit if the individual in question 
is encompassed by a defined -benefit pe nsion in accordance with the provisions of a collective bargaining 
agreement. Variable remuneration is, for senior executives apart from the Chief Executive Officer, only 
pensionable to the extent it is required pursuant to the applicable provisions of col lective bargaining
