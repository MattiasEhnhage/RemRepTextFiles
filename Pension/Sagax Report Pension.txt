Non-monetary benefits shall facilitate the senior executive’s work performance and 
correspond to what can be considered reasonable in relation to practice in the market

where the respective senior executive is active. Premiums and other costs (including costs 
for social security contributions ) for the employer in connection with such benefits may 
(when the benefit is resolved) amount to a maximum of 10 percent of the executives’ 
annual fixed remuneration. 
Senior executives’ pension terms shall be based on defined contribution pension solutions 
and follow or correspond to, and thus be limited to, the general pension plan under the ITP plan.
