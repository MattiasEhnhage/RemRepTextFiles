Pension benef its 
The senior executives shall be offered marketable terms and levels of pension 
benefits. The pension benefits shall be defined premium pension benefits, unless 
the senior executive is subject to defined benefit pension in accordance with the 
provisions of applicable collective agreement. Senior executives shall retire at the 
age of 65. Pension provisions shall be based on fixed cash salary only. Pension 
benefits may not amount to more than the equivalent of 15 percent of the fixed 
cash salary of the seni or executive, unless a higher provision is required by a 
collectively agreed pension plan.
