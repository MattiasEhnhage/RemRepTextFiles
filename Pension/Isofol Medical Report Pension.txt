Pension 
For the CEO, retirement benefits, including health insurance, are premium based and the 
premium shall not exceed thirty (30) per cen t of the fixed annual cash remuneration. For other 
members of the executive management, retirements benefits, including health insurance, are 
premium based, unless the executive manager is subject to a defined benefit contribution plan 
in accordance with s tipulations of a compulsory collective bargaining agreement. Premiums 
for premium based pensions shall not exceed thirty (30) per cent of the fixed annual cash remuneration. Variable remuneration shall not qualify for pension benefits.
