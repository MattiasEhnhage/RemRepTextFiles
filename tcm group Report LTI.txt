The long- term incentives are primarily designed to incentivise long- term performance, commitment 
and retention of members of the Executive Management, as well  Management, as well as to promote alignment of interests with those of the shareholders. The long- term incentives may potentially result in a maximum value 
per financial year of up to 50% of the executive’s fixed annual base salary. 
 The LTI programme consists of individual Performance Share Unit Plans commencing each year with 
a three- year performance period. At the end of the performance period, the performance share units 
may be converted into shares in TCM Group A/S, which will be granted free of charge. The commencement of each new plan is subject to a separate decision by TCM Group’s Board of 
Directors. The company intends to use treasury shares to meet its obligations to deliver shares under the Performance Share Unit Plan s. 
 The LTI programme is applicable for the performance period s 2022-2024, 2023-2025 and 2024-2026.
