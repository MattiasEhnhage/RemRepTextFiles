The objective of remuneration is to promote 
operations and the achievement of targets in line with 
Olvi plc’s purpose, strategy and values by maintaining 
and developing competitive, fair, motivating and 
rewarding systems and compensation. Olvi plc has 
created remuneration practices that encourage its 
people to promote the Company’s success and the 
creation of shareholder value over the long term. The 
CEO’s remuneration is also based on these practices.

Short-term incentives (STI) 
The goal of short-term remuneration is to encourage 
and reward the implementation of the short-term 
business strategy and the achievement of operational 
and financial targets. The company’s performance 
reward system communicates its level of ambition 
and targets. 
The grounds for incentives are determined annually 
by the Board of Directors. In the short-term incentive 
plan, the maximum monitoring period is one financial 
year, and the achievement of the targets is assessed 
in connection with the completion of the financial 
statements. The remuneration under the short-term incentive plan 
paid to the CEO in 2024 was based on the short-term
