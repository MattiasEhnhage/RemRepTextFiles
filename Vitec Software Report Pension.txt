Pension 
The agreed retirement age for the CEO is 65 years and there is no agreed retirement age 
for other senior executives. Pensions should always be premium based (defined 
contribution), in order to create predictability in terms of the company's future liabilit ies. 
Pension premiums are agreed individually, maximum up to deductibility. Other than the 
aforementioned pension benefits, the company does not have any other pension obligations for senior executives. 
 
Notice of termination and severance pay 
The employment or assignment contracts of senior executives shall be valid until further 
notice or for a fixed period of time.
