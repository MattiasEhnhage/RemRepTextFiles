2.2 Pension benefits 
For the CEO, pension benefits shall be a defined contribution scheme , and the pension 
premiums may amount to not more than 50 per cent of the annual fixed base salary. Variable 
remuneration shall not qualify for pension benefits. 
For other EMT members, pension benefits shall be a defined contribution scheme , and the 
pension premiums may amount to not more than 30 per cent of the annual fixed base salary. 
No current EMT members have a defined benefit pension . However, defined benefit pension

can be offered to future EMT members as part of a pre-existing agreement. To the extent that 
variable remuneration qualifies for pension benefits under the applicable collective bargaining 
agreement, the pension benefits shall be deducted from the payment and paid as pension.
