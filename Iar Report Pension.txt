Pension & other benefits 
The CEO is covered by a pension plan corresponding to the cost of the ITP plan, but with a retirement 
age of 60, which raises the premium compared with a retirement age of 65. Other employees are 
covered by a pension plan corresponding to the cost of the ITP plan. 
Other ben efits may include, for example medical insurance (Sw. sjukvårdsförsäkring), phone benefits, 
meal benefits and company car and shall be provided to the extent this is considered to be in line with 
market conditions.
