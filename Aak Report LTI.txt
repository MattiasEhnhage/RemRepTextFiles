2021/2026 and Incentive Program 2022/2027) for the executive management and other key 
employees. Incentive Program 2021/2026 has been replaced by Incentive Program 2022/2027. 
In addition, the company has implemented a performance and share based long -term incentive 
program for sen  i.e. a right to receive shares at a future time provided that applicab le

Performance Shares is linked to a performance condition set by the Board of Directors based on 
earnings per share for the 2023 financial year. If all of the conditions set out in Incentive 
Program 2023/2026 are met, half of the Performance Shares are transferred in the first half of
