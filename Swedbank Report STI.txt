Total remuneration in relation to the Guidelines 
A prerequisite for the successful implementation of the bank 's business strategy and safeguarding of 
its long -term interests, including sustainability, is that the bank is able to recruit and retain qualified 
personnel. Hence, the terms of employment for senior executives shall be designed with the purpose 
of ensuring the bank 's acce e risk 
assessments at Group level, business area level, team level and/or individual level. Further, the 
financial results of the relevant business area should be considered as well as individual performance 
targets based on financia l and operational factors. 
By meeting these criteria, the remuneration to the CEO and the deputy CEO contribute to the bank 's 
business strategy, long -term value creation , and sustainability. 
The CEO 's and the deputy CEO 's remuneration consist mainly of fixed salary. The fixed annual salary 
constitutes 7 7% for the CEO and 7 4% for the deputy CEO of the total annual remuneration in 202 4. 
The fixed salary is subject to an annual review to ensure a level that reflects the position 
responsibilities and nature , individual performance , and market competitiveness. The fixed 
remuneration also consists of benefits that constitute 0.16% for the CEO and 2.1% for the deputy CEO 
of the total remuneration in 2 024. The benefits package is unchanged du tors has the right to temporarily deviate from the
