Variable remuneration shall not qualify for pension benefits unless required by mandatory collective agreement provisions. The pension premiums for premium defined pension shall not amount to more than 25 per cent of the annual fixed base salary. Other benefits Other benefits may include, for example, life insurance, medical insurance (Sw: sjukvårdsförsäkring) housing allowance, school fees, travel cost compensation and company cars. Such benefits may amount to not more than 30 per cent of the annual fixed base salary. Pension benefits and other benefits may be duly adjusted for compliance with mandatory rules or established local practice depending on which law the employment is governed by, taking into account, to the extent possible, the overall purpose of this policy.
