enable the company to offer the CEO a competitive total remuneration. By establishing that variable compensation 
shall be linked to predetermined and measurable f inancial and non -financial criteria, the variable cash remuneration, 
together with Billerud's share- based incentive programs, promote Billerud's business strategy, long -term interests 
and sustainability performance.
