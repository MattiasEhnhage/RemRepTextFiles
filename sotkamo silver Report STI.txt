Compensation Report 202 4 
Introductio n 
This report describes how the guidelines for compensation to senior executives for Sotkamo 
Silver AB, adopted by the AGM 2024, was applied in the year 2024. The report also contains 
Information on compensation to the CEO. The report has been drawn up in accordance with 
the Companies Act and the College's for Svens K corporate governance rules on remuneration to the leader 
executives and on incentive programs. 
Further information on reimbursements to senior executives can be found in Note 4 (Personnel) on 
Pages 6 6–67 in the annual report for 202 4. 
Board fees are not covered by this report. Such fees are decided annually by the AGM and 
Reported in note 4 on pages 6 6–67 in the annual report for 202 4.
