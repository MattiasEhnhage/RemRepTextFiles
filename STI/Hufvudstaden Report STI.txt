 Drawing of bonus criteria 
Bonus shall be linked to predetermined and measurable criteria whose fulfillment shall 
measured annually. For the CEO may be a maximum of bonus with three fixed 
monthly salaries per year and for other senior executives with maximum the higher 
of three fixed monthly salaries and SEK 250,000 per person per year. The bonus part aims to give 
Group management a special incentive to work for the company's positive results and 
Thus, support the shareholder interest. The criteria for bonuses should be designed so that they 
Promotes the company's business concept and long -term interests, including sustainability. The criteria will 
to 70 percent consist of financial targets related to business results (operating profit 
excluding changes in value in the property portfolio and comparative -affecting items and 
With some other adjustments caused, among other things, by the responsibility of the executive holder -
area and position), to 15 percent of operational goals related to customer satisfaction and 
to Extension and 
to 15 percent of individual goals. 
 
When the measurement period for fulfilling criteria for the payment of variable cash setting 
terminated/determined to what extent the criteria are met. The Board is responsible 
for the assessment as far as variable cash is concerned to the CEO. For 
variable cash setting to other senior executives is responsible 
the director of the assessment. Regarding financial objectives, the assessment should be erased on 
The annual report by the company last announced annual report.
