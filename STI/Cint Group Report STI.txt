The board of directors may temporarily be entitled to depart from these Guidelines, 
in whole or in part, if in a specific case there is special cause for the departure and 
the departure is necessary to serve the Company’s long -term interests, including 
sustainability, or to ensure the Company’s financial viability. No such deviations 
were applied during the year.
