REMUNERATION REPORT 2024 | 4 Remuneration of the CEO
The Board of Directors determines the remuneration of the CEO. 
The Board considers Enersense’s strategy and long­term targets 
when setting the criteria and targets for remuneration. The crite­
ria selection aims to steer the CEO towards the implementation 
of the company’s strategy and the achievement of sustainable 
financial results.
