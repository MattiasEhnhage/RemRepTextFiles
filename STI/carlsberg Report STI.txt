Long-term incentive arrangements
The long-term incentives are designed to 
incentivise the executive directors to deliver on 
the long-term business objectives and to 
maximise alignment w e alignment of 
the executive directors’ award with the long-
term Group strategy and shareholder value. In 
order for any award (or part of an award) to 
vest, the Committee must be satisfied that 
underlying Group performance is at a 
satisfactory level.

priorities, including our ability to drive revenue 
and earnings growth through premiumisation,
