c) Other key terms applied to the employment relationship 
Pension arrangements can be made with the CEO, and it is possible to provide the CEO with signing bonuses, 
commitment bonuses and compensation for the termination of the employment relationship. 
The CEO’s pension arrangement must be payment -based, and the payment can be no more than 25% of the 
CEO’s annual salary. The retirement age applied in the pension arrangement must be based on the statutory 
pension scheme that covers the CEO. 
The CEO must, for the entire duration of the employment relationship, own a number of shares gained through 
remuneration schemes that is at least equal in value to his/her gross annual salary.
