Proposal by the Board of Directors of Sandvik AB for guidelines for the 
remuneration of senior executives 
 
The Board of Directors proposes that the Annual General Meeting resolve to adopt the 
following guidelines for the remuneration of senior executives. Compared to the guidelines 
adopted by the Annual General Meeting in 2020, the only amendment to these guidelines 
relates to the pension benefits, where the cap for defined contribution benefits has been 
lowered to be better aligned with market practice and the possibility to offer defined benefit 
schemes has been removed. 
 
Scope of the guidelines 
 
These guidelines encompass the President and other members of the Group Executive 
Management. The guidelines do not apply to any remuneration decided on or appr oved by 
the General Meeting.
