Short-term and long-term incentive systems 
Metsä Group has a short-term incentive system with a review period 
of one calendar year, as well as a long-term incentive system with a 
performance period of three years and a restriction period of one to two 
years. The targets of the long-term incentive system, tied to return on 
capital employed, operating result and equity ratio, impact the long-term 
development of shareholder value. With the support of the incentive 
systems, the Board of Directors steers Metsä Group’s operations over the 
short and long terms. Metsä Group
