The President & CEO is part of the bonus program which is based on reaching the annual targets set to the 
President & CEO. Targets are mainly based on the net sales and earnings targets. The target level of the bonus is 
20% of the CEO’s total compensation. The maximum bonus that can be received can total no more than 40% of 
annual salary. 
If the changes in the company’s economic conditions occur the Board of Directors can exceptionally cancel the 
bonus payments for that financial period. 
Correspondingly to approval of the CEO’s financial targets the Board of Directors will confirm annually the 
financial targets for the management team. 
 
Pension plans 
No other pension arrangements, beyond those mandated by law, will be made with the President & CEO.
