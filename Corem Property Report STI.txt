performance, linked to predetermined measurable criteria that are designed so that they promote the company's

The size of the variable compensation for 2024 is based on outcomes in relation to set goals, 
Both in relation to the company's results and in relation to individual performance. Payment is made in shape 
of salary, annually after the financial statements are determined. The compensation has a one -year performance and earnings period 
And maximum outcome for the annual result -based compensation amounts to a fixed annual salary for each year.
