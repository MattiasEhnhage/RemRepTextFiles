Remuneration policy and structure 
The overall purpose of the Remuneration Policy is to (i) 
attract, motivate and retain qualified members of the Board 
of Directors and the Executive Management, (ii) ensure the 
convergence between the interests of the Board of Directors 
and Executive Management and the interests of the 
company and the shareholders, and (iii) contribute to the 
promotion of long -term value creation in Gubra and thus 
support Gubra’s business strategy and sustainability . 
In accordance with the Remuneration Po  the Remuneration Policy, the 
remuneration to Executive Management consists of:
•Fixed annual salary
•Pension contribution
•Usual fringe benefits
•Variable short -term cash -based incentive
