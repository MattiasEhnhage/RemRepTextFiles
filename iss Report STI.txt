The purpose of the Remuneration Report (the 
Report) is to describe the remuneration of the 
members of the Executive Group Management 
Board (EGMB) and the Board of Directors. The 
principles outlined in the Remuneration Policy for 
EGMB also apply to members of the Executive 
Group Management.
The Report for 2024 is compliant with the Danish 
Companies Act. It is subject to an advisory vote at the 
Annual General Meeting in April 2025.

Remuneration Committee
The Board of Directors (the Board) has established 
a remuneration committee. The Committee 
provides oversight in preparing the Remuneration 
Policy. It evaluates and makes recommendations 
for the remuneration of the Board and the 
Executive Group Management Board (EGMB) 
members. It also approves the remuneration of the 
Executive Group Management (EGM).
The members of the Committee are Kelly Kuhn (Chair), 
Niels Smedegaard and Reshma Ramachandran.
Four meetings were held in 2024.
