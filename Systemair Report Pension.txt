Variable pay is based on the Company's 
performance with the aim of promoting the 
Company's strategy, long -term value creation and 
sustainability. The variable portion is paid as a 
proportion of the fixed salary and may amount to 
no more than 50 per cent of the annual salary for 
the CEO, 30 per cent for other Senior executives 
and 15 per cent for key individuals . 
As a principle, pensions shall be premium -based 
and shall not exceed 35 per cent of the wage base. 
The size of the pension shall adhere to the same 
criteria as above. 
The Board shall be authorised to depart from the 
guidelines if there are special reasons for doing so 
in individual cases .
