 5. Remuneration of the CEO and Deputy CEO 
 
Introduction 
The remuneration of Atria Plc’s management aims to promote the company’s long-term financial 
success and competitiveness and the favourable development of shareholder value. 
In 2024, the remuneration of the CEO and Deputy CEO consisted of the base salary (including fringe 
benefits), short -term incentives (STI), long -term incentives (LTI), pension and other benefits. 
According to the remuneration policy, the CEO’s variable remu neration corresponds to a maximum 
of 50 percent of the CEO’s maximum salary, and for the Deputy CEO, 40 percent of the Deputy 
CEO’s maximum salary.
