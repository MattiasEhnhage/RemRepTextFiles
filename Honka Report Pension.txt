Pension scheme 
 
The CEO’s pension cover has been organised with statutory pension cover that is based on length of 
service and earnings. The CEO’s retirement age is determined by the Employees Pensions Act. 
As part of the CEO’s overall remuneration, supplementary pension cover may be offered in addition to 
statutory cover. Any supplementary pension cover shall be implemented with defined contribution 
plans.
