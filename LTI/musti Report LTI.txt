The total remuneration paid to the CEO in FY2024 consisted of fixed monthly salary, fringe benefits, 
short-term bonus, and long-term incentive plan. The short-term bonus is to promote the Company’s 
strategy through the achievement of annual targets. The aim of a share-based incentive plan is to align 
the objectives of the shareholders and key employees for increasing the value of the Company in the 
long term. The remuneration paid and incentive plans to the CEO are disclosed under `Remuneration of 
the CEO´.
