The performance measures for the CEO’s short -term variable cash remuneration, which may not 
amount to more than MSEK 2 per year , have been selected to deliver the company’s strategy and to 
encourage behaviour which is in the long -term interest of the company. In the selection of 
performance measures, the strategic objectives and short -term and long -term business priorities for 
2022 have been taken into account. Also, the performance measures further contribute to alignment 
with sustainability as well as the company values.

Application of performance criteria for multi -year variable remuneration 
In addition to base salary and short -term variable cash remuneration, the CEO may receive an 
additional variable cash remuneration corresponding to a maximum of nine (9) monthly salaries. At 
the time of writing, there are no performance criteria regarding extra variable compensation for the 
CEO. Previous programs expired as of 2023.
