(1) Other benefits: Other bene cation of performance criteria 
The performance measures for the variable remuneration of the CEO are constructed to meet 
Investor’s overall purpose, execute the strategy, drive value creation, practice engaged long -term 
ownership and safeguard the development of Investor. 
The CEO’s short -term variable remuneration (STVR) is determined by achievement of pre -defined 
annual performance targets, financial as well as non -financial, qualitative and quantitative. 
Examples of target areas for the CEO, are deliverables linked to value creation plans for portfolio 
companies, achievements of sustainability targets and the over all development of Investor. 
Maximum STVR for the CEO equals 10 percent of fixed cash remuneration. For the present CEO, 
the outcome 2024 was 90 percent of maximu m STVR. For the former CE O, the average outcome 
for the last three years, has been 93 percent of the maximum STVR.
