Fixed market cash salary 
The fixed cash salary for senior executives shall be on market terms and be based on 
each individualâ€™s competence, responsibilities, experience and performance. The fixed 
cash salary shall be reviewed annually to ensure that it corresponds with market 
practice and remains competitive. 
 
Variable cash remuneration 
As regards variable cash remuneration, such compensation shall be tied to financial 
or non-financial concrete, measurable goals for the Company and/or the department 
that the manager is responsible for. Decisions regarding variable payment models and 
the outcome of such models shall be made by the Board of Directors. The variable 
cash remuneration may amount to not more than 50 percent of the fixed annual cash 
salary. Variable cash remuneration shall not constitute pensionable income.
