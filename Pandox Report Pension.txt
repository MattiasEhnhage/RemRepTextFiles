For the CEO and other executive managers, pension benefits shall be premium defined. The pension 
premiums for premium defined pensions, shall amount to not more than 35 per cent of the fixed 
annual cash salary. Variable cash remuneration shall not qualify for pension benefits. 
Other benefits may include, for example, life insurance, medical insurance (Sw: sjukvårdsförsäkring) 
and company cars. Such benefits shall not constitute a substantial part of the total remuneration.
